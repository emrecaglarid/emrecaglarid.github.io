Emre Caglar

I help build B2B experiences for|humans and agents. Currently a principal product designer at Supermetrics.

I've spent the last decade designing enterprise products, most of it at Supermetrics, where I joined as the second designer and now work as a principal IC, leading and coaching two designers on the company's most complex product surface while staying close to the work myself. For much of that time, I worked on complex data workflows for marketers and analysts at scale. Now my focus is agentic UX: how people delegate to, collaborate with, and build trust in AI agents.

My background is in industrial design research. My PhD was on design fiction, I built a world-building toolkit for foresight exercises about disruptive technologies, imagining systems under pressure to understand them better, and completed the research as a visiting researcher at Aalto University. That discipline still shapes how I think about complex product problems.

Based in Helsinki.
