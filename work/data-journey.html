Building the Data Journey

Five consecutive initiatives expanding the Supermetrics Hub from a platform with no users to ~€12M ARR.

Role
Principal Designer · Design Lead
Year
2021–2025
Scope
Data Blending · CDI · DWH Self-Serve · Storage 1.0 & 2.0
Company
Supermetrics

Supermetrics built its first decade on the Google Sheets sidebar. It was the product most users knew — pull marketing data from 350+ connectors, land it in a spreadsheet, done. Effective, but narrow. By 2021, the company had a new bet: the Hub. A platform product where data could be queried, scheduled, blended, stored, and activated, without ever opening a spreadsheet.

The problem was adoption. The sidebar had ten years of habit behind it. The Hub needed reasons to exist that the sidebar simply couldn't match.

The five initiatives in this case study were, in different ways, all answers to that question. I designed across all five, moving between sole IC, design lead, and team lead depending on the stage.

Together, these features helped take the Hub from €0 to ~€12M ARR, over 500 customers, 99% monthly logo retention, and 35% CAGR over two years.
